Characters in Noli Me Tangere

Read More

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra Y Magsalin
  • He is the son of the infamous Don Rafael Ibarra
  • Crisostomo is a youthful, studious young man who just came from studying in Europe after 7 years
Maria Clara De Los Santos y Alba
  • The daughter of a wealthy and powerful man from San Diego
  • She studied at Colegio de Santa Catalina de Sena for 7 years
Elias
  • A son who was initially described as arrogant and full of himself
  • He now helps those who experience injustice by the system
Kapitan Tiago
  • One of the most influential and wealthy people in San Diego
  • Known as a religious and holy man who constantly donates to the church
Padre Damaso
  • A Franciscan priest who used to serve in San Diego but due to circumstances he was transferred to another town
  • He is known to punish people by physically hurting them
Padre Salvi
  • He was the succesor of Padre Damaso
  • Unlike Padre Damaso who physically punish people, Padre Salvi punishes by imposing fines
Don Rafael Ibarra
  • The father of Crisostomo and the richest man in town before his death
  • A father who was wrongly accused of being a heretic and filibuster
Don Anastacio
  • An old man who roams around San Diego who is considered crazy and deranged by many uneducated.
  • He burries himself in books and is deeply inlove with the subject Philosophy
Narcisa
  • A young mother of two young sons
  • She was constantly abused by her husband who barely cared about their two children
Crispin
  • A young 8 year old boy who worked as a sacristan
  • He was accused of stealing two gold pieces or 32 pesos by the Sakristan Mayor
Basilio
  • A young 11 year old boy who worked as a sacristan along with this younger brother
  • He was the only one to survive after their work in the convent
Donya Victorina de Espadaña
  • She was an indio who was described to be ashamed of her own blood
  • She tried to do many things that Spaniards would have done in order for fellow Indios to look up to her and think highly of her
Don Tiburcio de Espadaña
  • A 35 year old Spaniard who worked as a custom officer
  • He decided to practice medicine, even though he had no experience in the medical field
Donya Consolacion
  • The wife of the Alperes, who used to be a Labandera
  • She loves to dress up and wear makeup
Alperes
  • He is the leader of the civil guards, who constantly had issues with Padre Salvi
  • He is described to be a drunkard and abusive to his wife and his people
Tiya Isabel
  • She is the cousin of Kapitan Tiago
  • After the mother of Maria Clara's death, she became a mother figure to her
